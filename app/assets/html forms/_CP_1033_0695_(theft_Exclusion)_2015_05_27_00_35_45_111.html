THEFT EXCLUSION
This endorsement modifies insurance provided under the following:
CAUSES OF LOSS – SPECIAL FORM
The following is added to the EXCLUSIONS section:
We will not pay for loss or damage caused by or resulting from theft.
But we will pay for:
1. Loss or damage that occurs due to looting at the time and place of a riot or civil commotion; or
2. Building damage caused by the breaking in or exiting of burglars.
And if theft results in a Covered Cause of Loss, we will pay for the loss or damage caused by that Covered Cause
of Loss.
